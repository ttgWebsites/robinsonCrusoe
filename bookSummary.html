Book Summary

  • So basically...
  • Robinson Crusoe is the tale of a young man who forgoes his father's dreams of him being a lawyer to pursue a life at sea. Crusoe nearly dies on his first trading trip, yet is not deterred and is determined to return to sea. On one voyage in the Caribbean, Crusoe is shipwrecked and washes up on an uninhabited island. He is alone for 28 years and manages to create a suitable living environment for himself. On the island Crusoe encounters cannibals, wild beasts, and other ship crews who arrive nearby near the end of the 28 years. Eventually Crusoe is rescued and manages to return to England. He is a wealthy man yet still yearns for the sea and goes on further voyages.
