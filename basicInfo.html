Basic Info

  • Title
  • Robinson Crusoe
  • Author
  • Daniel Defoe
  • Published
  • London, England. William Taylor, 1719.
  • Genre
  • Adventure story.
  • Setting
  • The novel covers the character Robinson Crusoe's adventures from 1659 through 1694 as he spends time in England, North Africa, Brazil, an island near Trinidad, England again, Libson, Spain, England once more, and lastly the island near Trinidad for a second time.
  • Point Of View
  • The novel is told in both first person and third person by Crusoe about his adventures and experiences.
  • Conflict
  • The major conflict occurs when Crusoe is shipwrecked on a deserted island. He must cope with loneliness, hunger, hardship, and eventually cannibals from a nearby island for many years before finally having the chance to leave the island.
